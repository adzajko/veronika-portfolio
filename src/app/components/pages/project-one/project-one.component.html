About Wheel friendly

This app is created to make disabled people's lives easier, to show you all the places you can easily go to from a cup of tea to the most beautiful museums

To help you find your dream job. Showing you all the careers that are wheelchair friendly and managers that will happily hire you!

This is a place of support where you can meet and talk to people with disabilities all around the world.
